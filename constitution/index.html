ALPFA Gotham City Chapter Constitution

Article I: Name

The name of this organization shall be the ALPFA Gotham City Chapter, a chapter of the Association of Latino Professionals For America, Inc.

Article II: Purpose

The purpose of this chapter is to support the mission of ALPFA by connecting Latino leaders for impact within the Gotham City area and empowering them through professional development, networking, and mentorship opportunities.
